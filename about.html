Our Story

Motherland Academy Nabweru is a leading educational institution in Uganda, dedicated to providing a holistic learning experience. We offer a robust curriculum that balances both Arts and Sciences, ensuring our students are prepared for the competitive world.

Our Mission

To nurture creative, responsible, and qualified citizens through quality education and discipline.

Our Vision

To be the leading centre of academic excellence and moral uprightness in the region.

Why Motherland Academy?

  • Qualified Staff We have a team of well-trained and dedicated teachers committed to every learner's success.
  • Top Facilities Our school features a well-stocked library, modern science laboratories, and spacious classrooms.
  • Spiritual Growth Religious activities and moral development are a core part of our student life.
  • Extracurriculars We excel in Music, Dance, and Drama (MDD) competitions at national level.
